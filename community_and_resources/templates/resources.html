Mental Health Programs at Monash

Monash offers a variety of free mental health programs which can teach you skills including stress management, study skills, suicide prevention and Mental Health First Aid. Such programs may enable you to improve your self care or help others who may be struggling in your community.

More information

Mindfulness

Mindfulness is a series of cognitive excercises which may help improve your mental state. Monash offers a range of mindfulness programs which may assist in managing stress, improving your academic performance, strengthening your relationship with others, and boosting self-esteem.
